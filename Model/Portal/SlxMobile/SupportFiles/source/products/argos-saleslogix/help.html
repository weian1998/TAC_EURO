Quick Tips

  • The configure button on the Home screen allows you to configure the "Go To" section.
  • You can expand and collapse panels to show/hide additional details where you see this icon.
  • Did you know that underlined text in detail views signifies a link action (such as browsing to a URL or jumping to an Account)?
  • The "Clear Storage" option under Settings is strictly a trouble-shooting device to clear locally stored configuration.

Quick Actions

  • Following phone call or email actions, a view will be displayed, allowing you to type notes and save to history.
  • The "View address" action will take you to the native map application on supported devices.
