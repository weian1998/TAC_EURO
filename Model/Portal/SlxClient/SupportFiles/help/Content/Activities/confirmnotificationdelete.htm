Activity Deletion Notification Detail View

When you are a member of an activity and another member deletes the activity, you receive notification of the deletion. The deletion detail view displays the activity time, regarding, notes, and the user who originally scheduled the activity, and allows you to delete the notification once read.

To work with a deleted activity notification

  1. Open the Activities List view.
  2. Click the Confirmations tab in the Activities view.
  3. Click the Delete next to the deleted activity notification you want to view.
  4. Read the activity information.
  5. Do one of the following:
  • To delete the confirmation response, click Delete.
  • To return to the Activity search view without deleting, click your browser Back button.
