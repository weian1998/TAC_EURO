Recurring Activities

Recurring activities take place at the same time each day, week, month, or year. Examples are a staff meeting every Monday at 10 A.M., or a board meeting at 9 P.M. on the second Monday of each month.

You can choose to have an activity occur:

  • once
  • daily
  • weekly
  • monthly
  • yearly

Depending on the option you choose, selections appear requiring more in-depth scheduling information.
