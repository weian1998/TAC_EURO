Events Tab

Use the Events tab on the Activities view to view and filter your scheduled events.

How Do I?

View events
  1. Open the Activities view and click Events.
  2. If necessary, use the filters to narrow the list.
  3. Click the event type to open the event.
View the summary pane
  1. Click Summary.
  2. To return to the List view, click List.
View or hide the details pane
  1. Click Detail.
  2. Select a record.
    The Details pane displays at the bottom of the list view with detailed information about the selected record.
  1. To hide the Details pane, click Hide Detail.
Open an event
  • Right-click the event you want to open and click Open Event

Delete an event

Schedule an event

  1. Do one of the following:

    • In the Task Pane Common Tasks, under Schedule, click Event.
    • Right-click in the grid and click Schedule Event.
  2. Follow the steps to schedule an event.
