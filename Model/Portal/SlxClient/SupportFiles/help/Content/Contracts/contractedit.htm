Editing a Contract

Use the Contract Detail view to edit information for an active or inactive contract. To undo any changes, click Reset before clicking the Save button.

To edit

  1. Open the Contract Detail view.
  2. Edit the contract information boxes as appropriate.
  3. Click Save.
