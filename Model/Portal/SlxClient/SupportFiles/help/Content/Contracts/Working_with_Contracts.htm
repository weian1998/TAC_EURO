What are Contracts?

Contracts are agreements that detail specific support after the sale of a product. A Warranty, for example, can become a contract to support a specific asset after the sale.

  • Use the Contract Detail view to add or edit information for a contract.
  • Use the Contract List view to view and manage contract groups.
