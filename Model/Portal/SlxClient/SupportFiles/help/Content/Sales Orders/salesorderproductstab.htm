Sales Order Products Tab

Use this view to add, edit, or remove products associated with the sales order.

How Do I?

Open this tab

  • Do one of the following:

  • On the Sales Order Detail view, click the Products tab. If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.
  • Add a Sales Order

Add a product

Edit product information

Delete a product

This view contains the following columns:

  • Edit - Click to edit the product information.
    This is only available from the Sales Order Detail view.
  • Use the Locked column or click the Lock All Items or Unlock All Items button to lock or unlock the product's price. (Accounting Integration Only)
  • Line # - Displays the line number assigned to each item on the sales order.
  • Product - Displays the product's name.
  • Family - Displays the product's family.
  • Price Level - Displays the price level used for this product.
  • Price (Base Currency) - Displays the price of the product. This column is based on the default currency type as defined by your administrator.
  • Discount - Displays the percentage by which the product was discounted.
  • Adj. Price - Displays the base price of the product, minus the discount. This column is based on the default currency as defined by your administrator.
  • Quantity - Displays the numeric quantity of the product added to the opportunity. Allows you to enter a new quantity.
  • Unit - Displays the scale used to measure the quantity of the product. For example, "Each" or "Box of 10". (Accounting Integration Only)
  • Extended Price (Opportunity Currency) - Displays the calculated total of the product price multiplied by the quantity, less any discount and converted to the opportunity currency type. (Multi-Currency only)
  • Extended Price (Base Currency) - Displays the calculated total of the product price multiplied by the quantity, less any discount. This column is based on the default currency as defined by your administrator.
