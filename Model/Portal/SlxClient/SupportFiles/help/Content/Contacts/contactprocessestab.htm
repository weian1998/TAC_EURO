Processes Tab

The Processes tab appears in the Contact Detail view. Use the Processes tab to manage contact processes attached to a single contact. The first two columns in the grid can contain graphic symbols that represent the type of the process or task or any options that are set.

To schedule a contact process

  1. From the Contact Detail view, click the Processes tab. If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.
  2. Click the Schedule Process button.
  3. In the Schedule For box, if the correct contact name is not the default, click the Find button and find the correct contact.
  4. In the Process Type list, click the drop-down arrow and select the desired process.
  5. In the Process Owner list, click the Find button and select the user who owns the process.
  6. Click Schedule.
