Editing a Defect

Use the Defect Detail view to edit information for an existing defect. For example, you can update the status, change the assignment to another person, or enter the details of how a defect was resolved.

To edit

  1. Open the Defect Detail view.
  2. Edit the defect information boxes.
  3. Click Save.
