Sharing Groups with Other Users

You can share your groups with other Sage SalesLogix users, teams, or departments.

Note the following items when you share groups with other users:

  • If you release a group to other users, and then delete the group, the group is no longer available to the other users.
  • When you make changes to a group released to other users, you must update the group so other users can receive the new version.
  • You can view access information for existing groups, to determine the owner and which users can view the group.
  • Users can see only the accounts, contacts, opportunities, and tickets to which they have access rights.
  • If an ad hoc group is released to other users, these users can add and remove group records.

  • If you share a Dashboard tab that contains a group, the group must also be shared with those users in order for the group to display on the Dashboard tab.

To share a group with other users
  1. On a List view, right-click a group tab.
  2. Click Share.
  3. Click the Add button.
  4. On the Assign Owner dialog box, click the Users tab.
  5. To limit the users listed, select a type of user from the User Type drop-down list (for example, Remote).
  6. Select the user(s) you want to share your group with, and then click OK.
To share a group with other teams
  1. On a List view, right-click a group tab.
  2. Click Share.
  3. Click the Add button.
  4. On the Assign Owner dialog box, click the Teams tab.
  5. To limit the teams listed, select the My Teams check box.

If you are logged in as Administrator, the My Teams check box will be unavailable.
To share a group with other departments
  1. On a List view, right-click a group tab.
  2. Click Share.
  3. Click the Add button.
  4. On the Assign Owner dialog box, click the Departments tab.
  5. Select the department(s) you want to share your group with, and then click OK.
To remove access to a shared group
  1. On a List view, right-click a group tab.
  2. Click Share.
  3. Select the user, department, or team you no longer want to share your group with.
  4. Click the Remove button, and then click OK.
To update a shared group

If you make changes to a group and want to share the changes, you must update the group.

  1. On a List view, right-click a group tab.
  2. Click Share.
  3. In the Share Group dialog box, click to OK release the changes to the listed users.
