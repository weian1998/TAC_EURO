Adding Your Personal Workspace Content

Use this view to add content that was removed or not previously displayed on your Personal Workspace.

To add

  1. Click the Personal Workspace tab where the content will be added.
  2. On the Personal Workspace menu, click Add Content.
  3. Select the check box for each content you want to add.
  4. Click OK.
