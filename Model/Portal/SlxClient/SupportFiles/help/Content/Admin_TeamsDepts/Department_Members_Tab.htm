Members Tab

The Members tab displays the users within a department. Use this tab to manage department members.

To manage

  1. In the Department Detail view, click the Members tab.
  2. Do one of the following:
  • Click Add to add a user to the department. Use the Lookup to find and select the department member.
  • Click the Remove link for the department member you want to delete.
  1. Click Save.
