Associations Tab

The Associations tab appears in both the Account and Contact Detail views. Associated contacts and accounts have a relationship with the selected contact or account.

Columns in the tab include: Contact, Relation, Description, Created by, and Date.

How Do I?

Open the Associations Tab
  • From the Contact or Account Detail view, click Associations. If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.
View the associated records
  • On the Associations tab, click the contact or account name.
Add an association
  1. Click the Add Association button.
  2. Follow the steps for adding a new contact or account association.
Edit an association
  1. Click the Edit link next to the record you want to edit.
  2. Follow the steps for editing a contact or account association.
Delete an association
  • Select the association you want to delete and click the Delete button.
