Package Products Tab

Use this view to add, edit, or remove products associated with the package.

How Do I?

Open this view
  • On the Package Detail view, click the Products tab.
Add products to a package
  1. Click Associate Products.
  2. Use the lookup to find the product you want to add.
  3. After selecting one or more products, click Add Selected.
Edit the product quantity

  • Select the record you want to edit, make your changes in the grid, and then click Save. To undo your changes, click Reset before you click Save.
    Not all columns can be edited in the grid. To determine if a column can be edited, click in the grid. If a white box appears, then the column can be edited.

Remove a product from a package
  1. Select the record you want to remove and click the Remove button.

  2. Click OK.

This view contains the following columns:

  • Product - Displays the product name.
  • SKU - Displays the Stock Keeping Unit (SKU) for the product.
  • Quantity - Displays the numeric amount of the product included in the package.
  • Status - Displays the product's status. For example Available.
  • Family - Displays the family to which the product belongs.
