Editing a Package Product

You can change the quantity of a product to be included in a package.

To edit

  1. On the Package Detail view, click the Products tab.
  2. Click Edit next to the product you want to edit.
  3. In the Quantity box, enter the amount of the product to be included in the package.
  4. Click OK.
