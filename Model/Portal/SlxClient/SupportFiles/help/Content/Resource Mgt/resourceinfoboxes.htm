Resource Information Boxes

The resource information boxes contain information about the selected resource. You can edit or delete the information as needed.

 

Box

Description/Action

Name

A brief description of the resource.

Type

The category of the resource (for example, conference room or white board).

Coordinator

 The person responsible for coordinating use of the resource. Click the Find button to find the person or team within your organization that manages the resource.

Coordinator Phone

The coordinator's phone number.

Coordinator Email

The coordinator's e-mail address. To send an e-mail message to the coordinator, click the E-mail button.
