Managing Resources

Only the system administrator and users with an administrator role can access these features.

You can add a resource, such as a conference room, to the list of available resources that appears when you schedule an activity.

How Do I?

Add a Resource
  1. On the Administration menu, click New Resource.
  2. Complete the resource information boxes.
  1. Click Save.
Edit a Resource
  1. On the Navigation Bar, click Administration, and then click Resources to open the Resources List view. If necessary, use the Lookup to locate a resource, and then click on the resource name.
  2. If necessary, change the resource information boxes.
  3. Click Save.
Delete a Resource
  1. On the Navigation Bar, click Administration, and then click Resources to open the Resources List view. If necessary, use the Lookup to locate a resource, and then click on the resource name.
  2. On the Resource Detail view, click the Delete button.

  3. Click OK.
