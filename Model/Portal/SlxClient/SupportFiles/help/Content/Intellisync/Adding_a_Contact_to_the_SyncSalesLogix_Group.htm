Adding a Contact to the SyncSalesLogix Group

You can add Sage SalesLogix contacts to the SyncSalesLogix group. By adding contacts to the SyncSalesLogix group any changes you make to the contacts are synchronized between Sage SalesLogix and Outlook or Lotus Notes the next time you run Intellisync.

To add

  1. Open the Contact List view.
  2. Select one or more records by pressing the CTRL or Shift key as you click each item. Right-click in the List view grid and select Add to an existing Group or from the Task Pane, select Add to Group, and point to SyncSalesLogix.
