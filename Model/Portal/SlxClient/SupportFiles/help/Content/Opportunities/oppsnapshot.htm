Opportunity Snapshot

You can view opportunity value and summary information about the selected opportunity in the Opportunity Snapshot in the top section of the Opportunity Detail view. You can edit the information as needed.

You can use the Copy Opp Info to E-mail hyperlink to send an e-mail with the opportunity information already entered into the e-mail body. The information copied into the e-mail includes the Opportunity description, Account name, Account Manager name, Reseller name, estimated close date, close probability, and comments. Additionally, it includes Product, Competitor, and Contact information, as well as value and summary information from the Opportunity Snapshot.

To copy Opportunity information into an e-mail

  1. From the Opportunities List view, click an Opportunity Description.

The Opportunity Detail view appears.

  1. In the Opportunity Snapshot box, click Copy Opp Info to Email.

Microsoft Outlook opens and a copy of the current Opportunity's information appears in the e-mail.

The information that appears in the Opportunity Snapshot depends on the opportunity status.
