Editing an Opportunity

Use the Opportunity Detail view to edit opportunity information. To undo any changes, click Reset before clicking the Save button.

To edit an opportunity

  1. On the Navigation Bar, click Opportunities.
  2. Find the opportunity you want to edit, and then click the description of the opportunity.
  3. On the Opportunity Detail view, edit the opportunity information.
  4. Click Save.
