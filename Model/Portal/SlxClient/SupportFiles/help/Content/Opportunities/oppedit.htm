Editing an Opportunity

Use the Opportunity Detail view to edit opportunity information.

To edit an opportunity

  1. On the Navigation Bar, click Opportunities, find the opportunity you want to edit, and then click the description of the opportunity.
  2. Edit the opportunity information.
  3. Click Save.
