Adding /Editing Opportunity Products

You can add opportunity products using the Lookup Product and Add Opportunity Product dialog boxes. The steps used to add and edit products depend on your access location.

When you add products and packages to an opportunity and accounting integration is:

  • Disabled - Adding a package adds all the products within the package to the opportunity as separate line items.
  • Enabled - The package/kit is added as one item. Each product within the package/kit is not added as a separate line item.

You must select an operating company for the account before adding products to an opportunity or sales order. (Accounting Integration Only)
To add or edit using the Lookup Product dialog box and Products tab

To add

  1. Open the Lookup Product dialog box.
  • From the Insert Opportunity view, in the Products area, click the Add Product button.
  • From the Opportunity Detail view, select the Opportunity Products tab, and click the Include Product button.
  1. If necessary, use the Filter Options to narrow the list of products.
  2. In the products list, select the product(s) you want to add to the opportunity.
  • Click Add Products to add the product(s) and keep the dialog box open.
  • Click OK to add the product(s) and close the dialog box.

To edit

  1. On the Opportunity Products tab, click the item in the grid that you want to edit, such as quantity, price, or discount.

The currency type and symbol are determined by the multi-currency settings.

  1. Click Save.
To add or edit using the Add Opportunity Product dialog box

To add

  1. Open the Add Opportunity Product dialog box.
  • From the Opportunity Options view, click the Products button.
  1. Use the Filter Options section to narrow the list of products in the Available Products section, and then click Show Results.
  2. In the Available Products list, select the check box of the product you want to add to the Selected Products grid, and then click Add.

Use the First, Previous, Next and Last buttons to scroll through a large list of products.

  1. Click OK.

To edit

  1. Click Edit to enter product information such as quantity, price or discount.

The Total field displays the total price of the products selected. The currency type and symbol are determined by the multi-currency settings.

  1. Click Save.
