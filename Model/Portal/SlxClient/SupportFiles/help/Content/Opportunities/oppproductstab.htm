Opportunity Products

Use this view to add, edit, or remove products associated with the opportunity. You can also change the product list order.

How Do I?

Open this view
  • This view appears on the Insert Opportunity view and as a tab on the Opportunity Detail view. If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.

Add a product to an opportunity

Edit opportunity product information

Remove a product from an opportunity

This view contains the following columns:

  • Line # - Displays the product's line number. This is useful when printing the order to ensure that the products are in the appropriate order.
  • Sort - Displays the product's sort order.
  • Product - Displays the product name.
  • Family - Displays the product family.
  • Price Level - Displays the price level used for this product.
  • Base Price (Base Currency) - Displays the price of the product. This column is based on the default currency type as defined by your administrator.
  • Discount - Allows you to enter a percentage discount.
  • Adj. Price - Displays the base price of the product minus the discount.  Allows you to enter a new adjusted price. If a new adjusted price is entered, the discount is recalculated to match the new amount. This column is based on the default currency type as defined by your administrator.
  • Price (Opportunity Currency) - Displays the product price converted to the opportunity currency type. (Multi-Currency only).
  • Quantity - Displays the numeric quantity of the product added to the opportunity. Allows you to enter a new quantity.
  • Unit - Displays the scale used to measure the quantity of the product. For example, "Each" or "Box of 10". (Accounting Integration Only)
  • Extended (Base Currency) - Displays the calculated total of the product price multiplied by the quantity, less any discount. This column is based on the default currency type as defined by your administrator.
  • Extended (Opportunity Currency) - Displays the calculated total of the product price multiplied by the quantity, less any discount and converted to the opportunity currency type. (Multi-Currency only).
