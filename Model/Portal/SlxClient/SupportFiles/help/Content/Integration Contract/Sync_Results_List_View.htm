Sync Results List View

The Sync Results List View displays information about synchronization cycles run between Sage SalesLogix and the accounting application. Only data related to the applying of records in the Sage SalesLogix or accounting system endpoint displays.

  • To sort the grid by a particular field, click one of the column headings.
  • To only display jobs that meet specific criteria, configure filters.

How Do I?

Open the Sync Results List View
  • On the Navigation Bar, click Administration, and then click Sync Results.
Open the Sync Result Detail View
  • Click the start time to open the Sync Result Detail view.

Use the Lookup to search for records
