Using Accounting Integration

Typically, a Sage SalesLogix user manages the integration of accounts and sales orders from Sage SalesLogix and your accounting system by:

  • Creating an account in Sage SalesLogix.
  • Linking the Sage SalesLogix account to a trading company in the accounting system.
  • Creating an opportunity for the account.
  • Creating a sales order for the opportunity.
  • Checking product prices.
  • Submitting the sales order to the accounting system.

After the sales order is submitted, the Accounting department manages the process. Transaction information displays in Sage SalesLogix for tracking purposes.
